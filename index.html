Our Work

What does Nk Engineering do? Put simply, we provide specialist mechanical services to asset owners in the oil and gas, power, generation, and industrial sectors. Our goal is to maintain the integrity of production and process plant and pipework - keeping your operations productive and safe. We believe that all leaks can be avoided - and that this is achieved through world class technical leadership, training, and innovation. Get in touch to find out about our leak free guarantee.
